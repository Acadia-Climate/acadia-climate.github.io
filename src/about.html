Our Story

We believe the work being done by developers today is essential in ensuring a better tomorrow

Acadia was born after witnessing a reforestation developer having to miss the annual planting season after failing to secure a bank loan backed by what the banks called “unbankable” carbon credit inventory. Witnessing the countless roadblocks developers face in securing financing, we are driven by a mission to close the massive gap in funding options available to the carbon removal economy compared to traditional, polluting industries.

We are motivated by the fact that most developers are Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) operating in developing countries, that in many instances, also distribute significant economic benefits to their project partners. Acadia supports developers and their project partners by providing fast and affordable credit solutions to fill the financing gap left by banks and other lenders who lack the agility and underwriting models to support an industry that needs to be urgently scaled.
