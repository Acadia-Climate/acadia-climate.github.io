Our Story

We believe the work being done by developers today is essential in ensuring a better tomorrow

Acadia was born after years of working with carbon removal project developers and witnessing them constantly running into roadblocks when trying to access the standard financing products. Carbon removal projects are often incredibly capital intensive, so the gap in the financing options available to developers today vs those normally available to traditional industries significantly is a significant source of friction. To empower developers, our mission is to bridge that gap and bring financing solutions expertly tailored to meet the needs of carbon removal project developers.
